1. Kabbalistic Tetragram
Kabbalistic tetragram (hexagram) or Masonic "Solomon's seal" depicts the devil as an equilateral triangle, equal to the first, but only facing the top down, rather than up, thus indicating the exact opposite Satan - God. Used to call evil spirits in the center of the hexagon traced on the ground.
2. Church of Satan
It is a symbol of Church of Satan in San Francisco. It is also located in the "Satanic Bible" by "Nine Satanic commandments." This symbol is always talking about the involvement in Satanism.
3. Cross Of Disorder
This symbol was first used by the Romans, who challenged the truth of Christianity. The symbol is a denial of Christian values and divine nature of Christ.
4. Pentagram/Pentacle
The five-pointed star is the most important symbol of magic. Typically, it is used by sorcerers and witches (WICCA), for "White Magic". For Kabbalists it becomes a symbol of power, omnipotence and autocracy Mind. This symbol like to use musical rock bands.
5. Inverted Pentagram/Pentacle
The inverted pentagram/pentacle, forming a head of the goat. It is on the cover of "The Satanic Bible". This is quite a serious character and almost always indicates involvement in Satanism.
6. Eye of Horus
Eye of Horus - all-seeing eye. One of the few characters belonging to Lucifer (as it is called followers - the king of hell). What is shown in the figure? Below the eye is a running down tear, it mourns those, who are outside his influence. In addition of the image of One Eye exists amulet Eye of Horus with image of hands, holding the bow of life or rod in the form of papyrus.
7. Ankh
As a symbol of inexhaustible vitality sign ankh was deposited on the walls of temples, monuments, and on the utensils. Ankh, the Coptic cross - a symbol, leading its origin from ancient Egypt. It was used as an amulet, prolonging life. The sign was used in the burial of the dead, because people believed that the dead awaiting the continuation of life, only in a different world. EP Blavatsky in the book "The Secret Doctrine" the ankh symbol described as a sign of the oath, life and covenant. Gods and Pharaohs on the images are often kept ankh in his hand or pass it to people.

It symbolizes the negation of all laws. Initially, the symbol "punk rock", but now it is also used in Satanism. This image can be seen on the walls in almost every city.

Symbol Star Aquarius (Universal hexagrams) - Emblem of Keepers of Knowledge, a symbol of great magic and power. The symbol appears in the form of six-pointed star, in some cases surrounded by two circles.

A hand with fingers folded into a goat horns: greeting among Satanists known as "satanic salute". Young people raise their hands at a rock concert like this. Generates head of the goat, a common symbol of Satanism. It is located on the back of "The Satanic Bible".
